FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is BeamCat used for?

BeamCat is an intelligent interaction tool based on gesture or eye control. It allows users to perform actions such as clicking, scrolling, and zooming without touching the mouse.

What devices does BeamCat support?

Currently, BeamCat only supports use on Windows. In the future, we plan to expand support to smartphones, TVs, air conditioners, washing machines, speakers, and other smart home

Can BeamCat be used without an internet connection?

Yes. Once installed, BeamCat can run completely offline. All tracking and gesture recognition are processed locally on your device to ensure speed and privacy.

Does BeamCat support customizable sensitivity and gesture settings?

Yes. BeamCat offers adjustable sensitivity settings. In future versions, we also plan to introduce customizable gesture configurations to better meet individual user needs.
